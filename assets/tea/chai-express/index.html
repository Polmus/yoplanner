Chai Express

Smooth, creamy and delightful, Chai Express is a well balanced blend of black tea and sultry spice. Crushed cinnamon, warm vanilla, with a hint of ginger and clove conjure up this aromatic chai reminiscent of an Indian street market. Excellent with a touch of milk and sugar.

Ingredients

  • Black tea
  • cinnamon
  • ginger
  • cardamom
  • clove
  • vanilla bean
  • natural flavour

How to prepare

1 tablespoon
3.5 mins
95 degrees

Caffeine
